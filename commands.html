Command reference

Every command the bot understands. Most of the time you won't need any of them — just send a link — but they're here when you want fine control.

Getting around

/startuser

Starts the bot and shows the welcome message. First stop for every new user.

/helpuser

The full in-bot help: basic usage, playlists, cookies, cleaning, settings, tags, links and quick commands — all in one message.

/languser

Switch the interface language. 25+ languages available including English, Hindi, Arabic, Russian, Spanish and Turkish.

/settingsuser

Opens the settings menu: quality, subtitles, keyboard, NSFW blur, cookies and everything personal in one place.

/usageuser

Shows your personal download history and statistics.

Downloading

URLjust paste a linkuser

No command needed — send any video or image URL and the bot downloads it. Up to 10 URLs in one message when quality-selection mode is active.

/audio/audio URLuser

Extracts the audio track from a video and sends it as a playable audio file. Supports playlist ranges too.

/vid/vid FROM-TO URLuser

Downloads a range of items from a playlist. /vid 3-7 URL = items 3 to 7, /vid -3-7 URL = counting from the end.

/playlist/playlist URLuser

Playlist download helper. You can also append *1*5 to any playlist URL directly.

/img/img [range] URLuser

Downloads images, photos and full galleries (gallery-dl). /img 11-20 URL grabs items 11–20, /img 11- URL grabs from 11 to the end. Up to 1000 files per run.

/link/link [quality] URLuser

Returns direct download links instead of the file. Qualities: 144–4320, or shortcuts like 720p, 1080p, 4k, 8k.

/list/list URLuser

Lists every format available for a link — resolutions, codecs, file sizes — so you can pick an exact format ID.

/searchuser

Opens inline video search via @vid. On desktop, just type @vid your query in any chat.

Quality & output

/format/format [144-4320] | 720p | 1080p | 4k | 8k | best | ask | id Nuser

Sets your download quality. ask shows a quality menu for every link; id 134 pins a specific format ID from /list.

/subs/subs [lang] | auto | offuser

Subtitle settings — download subs in your language, use auto-translated tracks, or turn them off. Embedded subs are limited to 720p / 500 MB / 1.5 h.

/split/split 100mb-2000mbuser

Splits large videos into parts of your chosen size — lossless cutting with ffmpeg.

/mediainfo/mediainfo on | offuser

Attaches full MediaInfo (codec, bitrate, resolution, duration) to downloaded files.

/tagsuser

Shows your saved tags. Add hashtags after any URL (URL #tag1 #tag2) and they'll appear in captions.

/keyboard/keyboard off | 1x3 | 2x3 | fulluser

Reply-keyboard layout with your favourite quick actions.

/nsfw/nsfw on | offuser

Toggles the spoiler blur on NSFW content.

Cookies & private content

cookie.txtsend as documentuser

The easiest way: export your browser cookies as cookie.txt and send the file to the bot. Private and age-restricted content unlocked.

/save_as_cookieuser

Paste cookie data as plain text and the bot saves it as your cookie file.

/cookie/cookie [youtube | tiktok | x | custom] [index]user

Downloads the bot's own cookies for a service. /cookie youtube 1 picks a specific source by index.

/check_cookieuser

Validates your cookies against a test video so you know they still work before a big download.

/cookies_from_browseruser

Extracts cookies directly from a browser profile.

Maintenance

/clean/clean [all | cookies | logs | tags | format | split | mediainfo | sub | keyboard]user

Cleans your data. Without arguments it removes media files only; /clean all wipes everything.

/uncache/uncache URLuser

Removes a URL from the cache so the next request downloads a fresh copy.

/proxy/proxy on | offuser

Routes your downloads through the bot's proxy pool (HTTP / SOCKS4 / SOCKS5) — useful for region-locked content.

/argsuser

Advanced: pass extra yt-dlp arguments for a download (power users only).

/add_bot_to_groupuser

Shows the benefits of adding the bot to a group — doubled limits, topic sorting, albums and more.

Admin only

/block_user/block_user <user_id>admin

Blocks a user from the bot entirely.

/unblock_user/unblock_user <user_id>admin

Lifts a block.

/ignore_user/ignore_user <user_id>admin

Silently ignores a user without notifying them.

/unignore_user/unignore_user <user_id>admin

Stops ignoring a user.

/ban_time/ban_time <user_id> <duration>admin

Temporary ban with an automatic expiry.

/log/log <user_id>admin

Fetches the activity log for a specific user.

/all/all <user_id>admin

Full user details: settings, history, limits state.

/reload_cacheadmin

Reloads the database cache manually.

/auto_cache/auto_cache on | offadmin

Toggles automatic cache reloading (default: every hour).
