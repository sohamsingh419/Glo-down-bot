Getting started

Three steps. That's it.

Open the bot

Tap the button below or search @YourBotUsername in Telegram and press Start.

Send any link

Copy a video, reel, track or album URL from your browser or app and paste it into the chat.

Get your file

Pick the quality if asked — the bot downloads, converts and uploads the file right into the chat.
