Content & privacy

Yes, with cookies. Export your browser cookies as cookie.txt and send the file to the bot (or paste them with /save_as_cookie). The bot then downloads as your logged-in account. Full walkthrough in the guide.

Cookies are stored per-user and used only for your own download requests — never shared, never shown to other users. That said, a cookie file is effectively a login key: only send it in the bot's DM, never in groups, and rotate it if you suspect a leak. /clean cookies deletes yours at any time.

Adult content is detected, tagged, and blurred with a spoiler by default (toggle with /nsfw on|off). In DMs it's a paid feature via Telegram Stars (1 ⭐ = $0.02). In groups it's free with full metadata. Illegal content is strictly prohibited and blocked.

In DMs, downloads are logged and cached in private log-channels — this is what makes repeat downloads instant. Group content is not logged or cached (configurable per group). You can wipe your data any time with /clean all.

Troubleshooting

The link needs its scheme: it must start with https:// or http://. Re-copy the full URL from your browser's address bar and send it again.

YouTube occasionally challenges automated requests. Fixes, in order: 1) send fresh cookies (export from a logged-in browser), 2) try /cookie youtube to use the bot's shared cookies, 3) wait and retry — challenges usually expire. The bot also rotates through multiple cookie sources automatically.

Cached links can go stale. Send /uncache URL to drop the cached copy, then resend the link for a fresh download.

Quick checklist: 1) Did you press Start? 2) Are you still a member of @YourChannel? 3) Hit a rate limit? Wait 60 seconds. 4) Still nothing — the bot may be restarting or down for maintenance; check the channel for status posts or contact @YourSupport.

Blocks happen for leaving the required channel, abusing rate limits, or attempting prohibited content. If you think it's a mistake, message @YourSupport with your user ID (from @userinfobot).
